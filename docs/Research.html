Research

My research currently focuses on both substantive and methodological interests. My substantive interests are in comparative political behavior and political economy, specifically looking at how electoral behavior is affected by economic institutions and features. I supplement this with methodological research questions focused on the ways we can improve our ability to estimate and interpret regression models with a particular emphasis on time-series, spatial, and time-series cross-sectional data and instrumental variable designs.
